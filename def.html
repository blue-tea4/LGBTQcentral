Sexualities

Sexuality is who you are atracted to in the simplest terms. People identify with the term they feel most comfortable using so the definitons might not include every specific use of it.

Gay

Someone who identifies as male atracted to another person who identifies as male. Some people also use gay as an umbrella term for the community.

Lesbian

Someone who identifies as female atracted to another person who identifies as female.

Bisexual

Someone who is attracted to two or more genders

Pansexual

Someone who feels atraction regardless of gender

Asexual

An umbrella term for someone who may not feel sexual atraction or is on the spectrum of that.

Aromantic

An umbrella term for someone who may not feel romantic atraction or is on the spectrum of that.

Gender

The socialy constructed characteristics of a person. This is how a person wants to present themselves. Not to be confused with sex, witch is what body parts a person has.

Transgender

Identifying with a gender that is not related to a person's sex at birth

Non-Bianary

Umbrella term for genders that dont line up with with the two most popular genders, male and female
